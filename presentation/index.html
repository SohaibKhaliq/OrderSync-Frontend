The Challenge

Running a restaurant is hard.
Scaling it across campuses is harder.

📋

Fragmented Operations

Orders, kitchen tickets, and billing live in disconnected spreadsheets and manual systems, causing delays and mistakes.

🧾

No Real-time Visibility

Managers can't see live order status, waitlist queue, or kitchen throughput without physically walking the floor.

👥

Poor Customer Experience

Long waits, no self-service options, and zero order tracking erode customer satisfaction and reduce repeat visits.

📊

Blind-spot Analytics

Without consolidated reporting, owners have no insight into best-sellers, peak hours, or revenue trends per location.

Security & Access Control

Always.

👤

Staff Roles

⚙️

Admin Roles

SETTINGS USERS INVOICES CUSTOMERS RESERVATIONS
🔒

Route Guards

PrivateRoute wraps all staff routes, ScopeProtectedRoute enforces feature-level RBAC, CustomerRoute guards customer-only sections.

🛡️

Session Security

Session persisted to localStorage under session_user. Helpers in UserDetails.js & AuthStatus.js for safe reads.
